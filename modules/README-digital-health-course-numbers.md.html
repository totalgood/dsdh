GLBH-40021: Data Science for Digital Health

Home / Courses And Programs / GLBH / Data Science for Digital Health

The Data Science for Digital Health course focuses on understanding innovative methods, tools and processes to transform data to knowledge and provide a foundation for measurable action. Data Science is being used for a variety of healthcare applications including predicting population health trends, utilization in medical products and solutions, and integration of data with healthcare systems. This course will additionally examine how data is utilized from the perspective of the patient.

Course Number: GLBH-40021 Credit: 3.00 unit(s) Related Certificate Programs: Digital Health
